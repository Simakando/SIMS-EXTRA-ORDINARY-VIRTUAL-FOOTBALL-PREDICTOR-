🛡️ UNDER-FILTER SYSTEM — ALL 12 FILTERS EXPLAINED
Applied AFTER signal gate passes. Each filter checks for signs that the game will go UNDER despite signal qualification.
3+ filters triggered → match BLOCKED from prediction
2 filters triggered → match flagged as CAUTION
0–1 filters triggered → match APPROVED
🎯 F1: Market Odds Check
Under 3.5 market odds below 1.45 = bookies strongly back under. Over 3.5 odds above 2.8 = low probability priced in. Blocked when market heavily disagrees with signal.
🏰 F2: Dominant Favorite
Home win odds below 1.25: heavy favorites tend to control pace and score efficiently without high goal counts. Big favorites reduce end-to-end play.
📊 F3: Marginal Signal Rate
When avg signal rate is below 58%, the over-signal is too weak. Only slightly above threshold means marginal edge — under outcomes are equally likely.
🔵 F4: Support-Only Signals
If zero primary signals (Team/H2H/League) qualified and only support signals (Odds Range/Draw/Underdog), the evidence is circumstantial — under risk is elevated.
⚽ F5: H2H Low Goals History
When a Head-to-Head signal exists showing avg goals below 2.8 between these teams, historical pattern contradicts the over prediction.
🏆 F6: League Low Average
When the matched league pattern shows avg goals below 2.5, this league plays tight regardless of individual team signals.
🤝 F7: Close Draw Odds
Draw odds below 2.8 suggest a closely contested match expected. Tight games where a draw is expected produce fewer goals on average.
⚖️ F8: Even Money Trap
Both teams priced between 1.7–2.4: evenly matched games statistically produce more tactical, goal-cautious play. Even money traps low scoring.
🔢 F9: Scoreline Conflict
If a Scoreline signal exists for this match and it shows an under-3.5 common result (e.g. 1-1, 2-0, 1-0, 0-0), it directly contradicts the over prediction.
🎰 F10: Signal Conflict Count
When more than half the matched signals show rates below 60%, there are more weak signals than strong ones. Low signal cohesion = unreliable over pick.
📉 F11: Confidence/Rate Mismatch
When confidence is 80%+ but avg signal rate is below 62%: false confidence inflated by signal count rather than rate quality. Misleading confidence badge.
🛡️ F12: Defensive Teams Check
If both home AND away team signals (when available) show over35 rates below 55%, both teams trend toward low-scoring games regardless of other signals.
📡 HOW THE SIGNAL ENGINE WORKS
1. Signal Detector — Scans Kiron VFL API for historical results across 7+ virtual leagues. Patterns qualifying at ≥30 matches are saved to IndexedDB (persist through phone restarts). Signals auto-prune if accuracy drops below 40% after 50+ tracked results. Best performers (≥70% hit rate, ≥50 matches) highlighted in gold.
2. Predictor — Fetches BetPawa upcoming rounds (MD+2 ahead). Evaluates each match against qualified signals.
3. Under-Filter — 12-point filter system removes matches that signal "over" but are likely to go under.
🏆 Best Performers
Signals with ≥70% hit rate AND ≥50 matches highlighted in gold. These are the most reliable signals and get priority weighting in confidence calculations.
🗑️ Auto-Pruning
Signals tracked via ML. If a signal type generates 10+ failures and its real-world accuracy drops below 40%, it is automatically pruned from the database.
💾 Persistent Storage
All signals, match data, and patterns stored in IndexedDB (survives phone restart, app close, cache clear). Signals grow smarter over time.
📅 MD+2 Mode
Now predicting 2 matchdays ahead instead of 3. Example: If current play is MD5, predictions shown for MD7. More accurate time window for Kiron VFL rounds.
📐 SIGNAL QUALIFICATION RULES
Pattern must pass ALL of these to qualify:
✅ Minimum 30 match occurrences across all VFL leagues
✅ Over 3.5 hit rate ≥ 50% (adjusts via ML)
✅ Signal strength ≥ 40 (composite of rate + sample)
⭐ Top Performer: rate ≥ 70%, occurrences ≥ 50
🔥 Strong: rate ≥ 65% → triggers OVER 3.5 bet
📊 Moderate: rate 55–65% → triggers OVER 2.5 bet
❌ Weak: rate < 50% → not used + flagged for pruning
